FAQ

The questions owners actually ask.

Do I have to change my phone number?+

No. You set a simple call-forward on no-answer, a standard feature on every line. Your number and your phone stay exactly as they are. Rebound only steps in when nobody picks up.

Will it sound like a robot?+

No. You give it your greeting, services and common questions, and it replies in natural language in your business's voice. You see every message and can take over any conversation instantly.

What happens if I do answer the call?+

Nothing changes. Rebound only reacts to calls you miss. If you pick up, it stays out of the way completely.

How fast can I be up and running?+

About ten minutes. Connect your number, turn on forwarding, set your greeting, done. We will walk you through it on a short call if you want.

What about my customers' data?+

All data is stored on EU servers and processed under a proper data processing agreement. Privacy is a first-class feature, not an afterthought.
