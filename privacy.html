This policy explains what personal data we process when you visit homeservicereply.com or contact us, why, and what rights you have under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). It covers this website. Personal data we process on behalf of our business customers (for example callers' details inside the service) is governed by our Data Processing Agreement.

2. What we collect, why, and on what legal basis

a. Server and access logs

When you load this site, our hosting provider automatically processes technical data your browser sends: IP address, date and time, the page requested, referrer, and user agent. This is necessary to deliver the site securely and to detect abuse. Legal basis: our legitimate interest in a secure, functioning website (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).

b. Web fonts

We load fonts from Google Fonts to display the site. When fonts load, your IP address is transmitted to Google. Legal basis: legitimate interest in a consistent presentation (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR). If you prefer not to transmit data to Google, we can self-host the fonts on request, and we recommend doing so before active marketing.

c. Waitlist and contact

If you submit your email to join the early-access list, or contact us, we process the email address and any message you send in order to respond and to keep you informed about the launch. The form is delivered through our form provider. Legal basis: steps taken at your request prior to a contract and our legitimate interest in responding to enquiries (Art. 6(1)(b) and (f) GDPR), or your consent where you opt in (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR).

d. Cookies

This website does not set tracking or advertising cookies and does not run analytics. Only strictly necessary technical processing described above takes place. If this changes, we will update this policy and request consent where required.
